G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering (GHRCE), Nagpur

Full Name: G.H. Raisoni College of Engineering, Nagpur
Location: Nagpur, Maharashtra, India
Established: 1996
Institute Type: Private Engineering College
Affiliated to: Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RTMNU)
Campus: Urban, approximately 35 acres
Hostel Facilities: Yes, Separate hostels for male and female students

Courses Offered:
1. Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech) in various branches:
   - Civil Engineering
   - Computer Science Engineering
   - Electrical Engineering
   - Mechanical Engineering
   - Electronics & Communication Engineering
   - Information Technology
   - Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
   - Electronics Engineering

2. Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech) in various branches:
   - Structural Engineering
   - Power Systems
   - VLSI Design
   - Thermal Engineering

3. Management Programs (MBA)

Notable Achievements:
- Accredited by NBA and NAAC.
- Strong placement records with top recruiters like TCS, Wipro, Cognizant, Accenture, and more.
- Active participation in research, with various projects funded by the government and industry.
- Numerous student clubs and associations for cultural and technical activities.
- Strong emphasis on entrepreneurship and innovation.
