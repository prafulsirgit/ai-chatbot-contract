Yashwantrao Chavan College of Engineering (YCCE), Nagpur

Full Name: Yashwantrao Chavan College of Engineering, Nagpur
Location: Nagpur, Maharashtra, India
Established: 1984
Institute Type: Private Engineering College
Affiliated to: Rashtrasant Tukadoji Maharaj Nagpur University (RTMNU)
Campus: Urban, approximately 30 acres
Hostel Facilities: Yes, Separate hostels for male and female students

Courses Offered:
1. Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech) in various branches:
   - Civil Engineering
   - Computer Science Engineering
   - Electrical Engineering
   - Mechanical Engineering
   - Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering
   - Information Technology
   - Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

2. Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech) in various branches:
   - Structural Engineering
   - VLSI Design & Embedded Systems
   - Thermal Engineering

3. MBA Program for students interested in management studies.

Notable Achievements:
- Accredited by NBA (National Board of Accreditation).
- Strong industry connections leading to good placement records in top companies such as TCS, Infosys, Cognizant, etc.
- Active student clubs, technical workshops, and seminars.
- Strong focus on research and innovation with various funded projects.
